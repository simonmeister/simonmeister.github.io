Biography

My goal is to make deep networks learn more effectively, approaching the performance of animal brains. I am currently particularly interested in lifelong learning, memory systems, meta-learning, top-down attentional control, as well as mechanisms that naturally lead to the emergence of higher-level concepts & compositionality.
